VirtualMic Audio Driver

This installer will add a VirtualMic virtual microphone to your Mac along with a companion app.

What gets installed:

  • VirtualMic driver — a virtual microphone input device
  • VirtualMic.app — GUI app for managing the proxy, playing sounds, and settings
  • VirtualMicCli — command-line tool for scripting and automation

After installation, open VirtualMic from your Applications folder and select a real microphone to proxy through the virtual device.

The driver runs entirely in user space. No kernel extension is installed.
